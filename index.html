About Footprints

Who we are

Footprints Press is an independent publishing house that was established in 2010 and is based in Kenya. The Publishing House takes its name from the renowned poem by Henry Wadsworth Longfellow, ‘Psalm of Life’.
Our books are exciting, innovative and enduring publications that inspire readers and contribute positively to the transforming landscape of the African continent. Whether through essays, poetry, photography, fiction or non-fiction work, the books celebrate Africa’s imagination, innovation, its past and its unfolding future through the authentic voices of the African experience.

What we do

The books are compilations that are typically created by the pulling together of several hands to make the whole. More like a montage, the creations are entire projects and can be likened to a child raised, loved, and owned by an entire village.
The books bear a distinctive appearance: large, bold colour, high quality, and designed for aesthetic resonance. They make thoughtful gifts and unique keepsakes.
